## AI Health Assessment

Get personalized health insights based on your symptoms.

### How It Works

**1. Start Assessment**
- Tap **AI Assessment** from Dashboard or bottom navigation
- Tap **Start New Assessment**

**2. Describe Your Symptoms**
- Select your symptom category OR describe your symptoms
- Be as detailed as possible
- Include when it started
- Example: "I have a headache and fever that started yesterday"

**3. Duration & Severity**
- Select how long you've had symptoms (Hours, Days, Weeks, Months)
- Specify the exact duration
- Rate severity: 1-3 (mild), 4-6 (moderate), 7-10 (severe)

**4. Review Results**
You'll receive:
- AI-generated health insights
- Severity classification
- Recommended actions
- When to seek medical attention
- Self-care suggestions

### Important Notes

‚ö†Ô∏è **This is NOT a medical diagnosis**
- Always consult a healthcare professional for serious concerns
- For emergencies, call 911 immediately
- Use this tool as guidance only

> **Tip**: All assessments are automatically saved. Find them in AI Assessment ‚Üí History. You can share results with healthcare providers if needed.

## Troubleshooting

### Sign-In Issues

**Can't sign in?**
- ‚úÖ Check email and password are correct (case-sensitive)
- ‚úÖ Ensure internet connection is working
- ‚úÖ Try **Forgot Password** to reset
- ‚úÖ Clear app cache and try again

**Forgot password?**
1. Tap **Forgot Password?** on sign-in screen
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for OTP (One-Time Password)
4. Enter the OTP in the app
5. Create a new password
6. Confirm your new password
7. Tap **Reset Password**

### Notification Problems

**Not receiving notifications?**
- ‚úÖ Check device notification settings (outside app)
- ‚úÖ Verify notifications enabled: **Profile** ‚Üí **App Settings** ‚Üí **Notifications**
- ‚úÖ Disable "Do Not Disturb" mode
- ‚úÖ Restart the app

### Location & GPS Issues

**Location not accurate?**
- ‚úÖ Enable location services in device settings
- ‚úÖ Grant "Always" permission for emergency features
- ‚úÖ Ensure GPS is enabled
- ‚úÖ Go outside or near windows for better GPS signal
- ‚úÖ Restart your device

### Syncing Problems

**Data not syncing?**
- ‚úÖ Check internet connection
- ‚úÖ Force sync: Pull down on Dashboard
- ‚úÖ Check storage space on device
- ‚úÖ Update app to latest version
- ‚úÖ Sign out and sign back in

### App Crashes

**App freezing or crashing?**
1. Update to latest version (App Store/Google Play)
2. Clear app cache
3. Restart your device
4. Uninstall and reinstall (your synced data is safe)
5. Contact support if problem continues
